<p><p>Ceramic Gas Discharge Tubes (GDTs) are critical components used for overvoltage protection in electronic circuits. The market for GDTs is segmented into two main types: Surface Mount Gas Tube Arresters and Through Hole Gas Discharge Tubes. Surface Mount GDTs are designed for modern circuit boards, offering compact dimensions and ease of assembly. In contrast, Through Hole GDTs feature leads that penetrate the circuit board, providing robust physical stability, typically suited for traditional or larger designs. Both types effectively safeguard sensitive electronic devices.</p></p>
