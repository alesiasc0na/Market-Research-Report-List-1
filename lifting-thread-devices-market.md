<p><p>Lifting thread devices are categorized into two main types: resorbable and permanent. Resorbable lifting threads gradually dissolve in the body over time, providing a temporary lifting effect while stimulating collagen production for natural rejuvenation. These are ideal for patients seeking less invasive options with minimal long-term commitment. In contrast, permanent lifting threads remain in place indefinitely, offering sustained support and lift, suitable for individuals wanting enduring results. Both types cater to varying aesthetic preferences and treatment needs in the cosmetic market.</p></p>
