<p><p>The structural steel fabrication service market includes various shapes used in construction and engineering. I-Beams are widely utilized for their strength and stability in supporting loads, while Angle (L-shape) beams offer versatility in framing and bracing. Hollow Structural Sections (HSS) provide aesthetics and strength, ideal for both structural and architectural applications. Z-Shape beams are used for purlins in roofing systems, while T-Shaped beams are employed in cross-sectional load-bearing applications, each serving distinct structural needs in projects.</p></p>
