<p><p>The High Voltage Busbar Protection Devices market comprises two primary types: Low Impedance and High Impedance devices. Low Impedance High Voltage Busbar Protection Devices operate by measuring the current flowing through the busbar, utilizing differential protection techniques, and are effective for detecting faults with minimal sensitivity to external influences. In contrast, High Impedance High Voltage Busbar Protection Devices utilize a high resistance configuration, allowing them to accurately detect faults while minimizing the risk of false tripping caused by external currents, improving reliability in substations.</p></p>
