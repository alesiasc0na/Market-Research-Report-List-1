<p><p>The seawater desalination pump market includes various types suited for different applications. Double-flow axial split case pumps provide efficient flow rates, and submersible motor pumps are suitable for underwater installations. Vertical volute pumps are utilized for their compact design, and vertical line shaft pumps serve deepwater applications.
