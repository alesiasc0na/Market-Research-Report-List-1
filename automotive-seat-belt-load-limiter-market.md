<p><p>The automotive seat belt load limiter market consists of three main types: </p><p>1. **Digressive Load Limiters** reduce force progressively with increasing load, enhancing passenger safety during moderate impacts by gradually deploying.</p><p>   </p><p>2. **Progressive Load Limiters** apply a consistent force increase corresponding to the load, providing controlled energy absorption during crashes.</p><p>3. **Adaptive/Switchable Load Limiters** dynamically adjust their response based on crash severity, optimizing safety for various impact scenarios, allowing for tailored protection.</p></p>
