<p><ul><li>Single Sided Probing Tester</li><li>Dual Sided Probing Tester</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flying probe tester market is divided into two main types: single-sided probing testers and dual-sided probing testers. Single-sided probing testers are designed to test one side of a printed circuit board (PCB), offering a cost-effective solution for simpler designs. In contrast, dual-sided probing testers can access both sides of a PCB, providing thorough testing capabilities for more complex assemblies. This distinction caters to varying testing needs, with each type delivering specific advantages in efficiency and accuracy for electronic manufacturers.</p></p>
