<p><p>The manual tile cutter market is categorized into two main types: single track and double track manual tile cutters. Single track manual tile cutters are designed with a single rail system, allowing for straightforward and efficient cutting of tiles with minimal setup. In contrast, double track manual tile cutters feature dual rail systems, providing enhanced stability and precision for larger or thicker tiles. Both types cater to different user needs, balancing affordability and functionality for diverse tiling projects.</p></p>
