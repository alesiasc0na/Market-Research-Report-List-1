<p><strong>The Automotive Wheel Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inner-rotor Motor</li><li>External-rotor Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive wheel motor market features two primary types: inner-rotor motors and external-rotor motors. Inner-rotor motors have the rotor located inside the stator, providing compactness and efficiency, often favored for their high torque and responsive performance in drivetrains. In contrast, external-rotor motors place the rotor outside the stator, offering advantages in cooling and a larger surface area, which enhances efficiency and power output. Both types contribute to the trend towards electric vehicles and improved vehicle dynamics.</p></p>
