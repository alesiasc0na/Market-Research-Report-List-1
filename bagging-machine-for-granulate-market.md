<p><p>A bagging machine for granulate is designed to efficiently package granular materials into bags for various industries. The V-FFS (Vertical Form Fill Seal) market focuses on machines that create bags from a flat roll of film, filling and sealing them in a continuous motion, ideal for high-speed production. In contrast, the H-FFS (Horizontal Form Fill Seal) market involves horizontal bag formation, suitable for larger or bulkier products. Both types enhance operational efficiency and reduce manual labor in packaging processes.</p></p>
