<p><ul><li>Pre-vacuum Autoclave</li><li>Steam Flush Autoclave</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steam autoclave market encompasses various types designed for sterilization. Pre-vacuum autoclaves utilize a vacuum system to eliminate air pockets, ensuring optimal steam penetration for effective sterilization. This method is particularly effective for complex instruments. In contrast, steam flush autoclaves operate by rapidly injecting steam into the chamber, followed by a vacuum phase to enhance sterilization efficiency. Both types are crucial in healthcare and laboratory settings, providing reliable methods for ensuring the safety and sterility of medical instruments.</p></p>
