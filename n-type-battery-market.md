<p><p>The N-Type battery market comprises two main types: Monocrystalline Bifacial and Monocrystalline Mono-side. Monocrystalline Bifacial panels capture sunlight from both sides, enhancing energy generation and efficiency by reflecting light from the ground. This design benefits installations with reflective surfaces. In contrast, Monocrystalline Mono-side panels are optimized for light absorption from only one side, offering a simpler installation process and lower costs. Both types exhibit high performance and longevity, contributing to the growing demand for advanced solar technologies.</p></p>
