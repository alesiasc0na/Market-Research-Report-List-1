<p><p>The Industrial Pearlescent Pigment market is primarily divided into two types: natural mica-based and synthetic mica-based pigments. Natural mica-based pigments are derived from naturally occurring mica minerals, providing a shimmering, reflective quality that is highly sought after in cosmetics, coatings, and plastics. Synthetic mica-based pigments, on the other hand, are artificially created to mimic the properties of natural mica, often offering greater consistency, superior performance, and a wider range of colors while being free from impurities. Both types play critical roles in various industrial applications.</p></p>
