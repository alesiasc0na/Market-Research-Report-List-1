<p><p>The serrated face nut market is categorized into two main types: single-sided serrated nuts and double-sided serrated nuts. Single-sided serrated nuts feature serrations on one face, providing enhanced grip and preventing loosening in applications where only one side is accessible. In contrast, double-sided serrated nuts have serrations on both faces, offering superior holding power and flexibility in installation. Both types are utilized in various industries, including automotive and construction, due to their reliability and ability to resist vibrations.</p></p>
