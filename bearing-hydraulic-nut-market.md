<p><p>The Bearing Hydraulic Nut market includes three primary types: Shim Type Nuts, Lower Collar Nuts, and Upper Collar Nuts. Shim Type Nuts feature adjustable shims for precise load distribution, making them suitable for various applications. Lower Collar Nuts are designed to be installed beneath bearings, providing stability and support. Upper Collar Nuts, on the other hand, are positioned above bearings to secure them in place. Each type enhances efficiency, reliability, and ease of maintenance in mechanical assemblies.</p></p>
