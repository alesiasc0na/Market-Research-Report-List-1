<p><p>The specialty grade carbon black market is categorized into several types based on production methods and characteristics. Furnace black, the most widely used, offers high performance in rubber and coatings. Channel black, produced by channel processes, is suitable for inks and paints. Thermal black is derived from the incomplete combustion of hydrocarbons, providing excellent conductivity. Acetylene black is known for its high purity and used in batteries and electronics. Other types may include lamp black and peat black, catering to niche applications.</p></p>
