<p><strong>The PP (Polypropylene) Pipe Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PP-R/RCT Pipe</li><li>PP-H Pipe</li><li>PP-B Pipe</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polypropylene (PP) pipe market includes several key types: PP-R (Random Copolymer) pipes, known for their exceptional resistance to temperature and pressure, are commonly used in hot and cold water systems. PP-RCT (Raised Temperature) pipes offer enhanced strength for high-temperature applications, making them ideal for industrial uses. PP-H (Homopolymer) pipes are robust and cost-effective, suitable for non-pressure applications. Additionally, PP-B (Block Copolymer) pipes provide better impact resistance and flexibility, serving diverse plumbing and drainage needs.</p></p>
