<p><p>The auto-dimming mirror market is categorized into mechanical and electrodynamic types. Mechanical auto-dimming mirrors use a simple adjustment mechanism that alters the angle of the mirror to reduce glare from headlights. In contrast, electrodynamic mirrors rely on electrochromic technology, allowing them to automatically darken when exposed to bright light sources, enhancing visibility and safety. Both types aim to improve driver comfort and enhance road safety by minimizing glare during nighttime driving conditions.</p></p>
