<p><p>The cloud server market comprises three main types: IaaS, PaaS, and SaaS. IaaS provides virtualized computing resources over the internet, enabling businesses to scale infrastructure as needed. PaaS offers a platform allowing developers to build, test, and deploy applications without managing the underlying hardware. SaaS delivers software applications through the cloud on a subscription basis, eliminating the need for installation and maintenance. Together, these models cater to diverse business needs, enhancing flexibility, cost efficiency, and scalability.</p></p>
