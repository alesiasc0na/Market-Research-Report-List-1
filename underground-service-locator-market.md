<p><strong>The Underground Service Locator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Location</li><li>Passive Location</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Underground Service Locator market is divided into two primary types: active location and passive location. Active location involves using electronic devices that emit signals to identify and trace underground utilities, providing precise locations. In contrast, passive location relies on existing infrastructure and natural phenomena, such as electromagnetic fields, to detect utilities without the need for signal generation. Both methods are essential for minimizing risks during construction and maintenance activities, ensuring safer and more efficient underground utility management.</p></p>
