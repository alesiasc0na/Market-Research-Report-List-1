<p><p>The LNG commercial vehicle market consists primarily of two types: spark ignition and hybrid compression ignition vehicles. Spark ignition vehicles utilize LNG as fuel, igniting the gas with a spark plug, providing efficient combustion and lower emissions. In contrast, hybrid compression ignition vehicles combine LNG with traditional diesel engines, enhancing fuel efficiency and performance while reducing pollutants. Both types cater to the growing demand for cleaner transportation solutions, adapting to various operational needs in commercial settings and promoting sustainability in the logistics sector.</p></p>
