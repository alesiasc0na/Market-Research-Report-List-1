<p><strong>The Cast Polyurethane Prepolymer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polyester Based</li><li>Polyether Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cast polyurethane prepolymer market comprises two main types: polyester-based and polyether-based. Polyester-based prepolymers offer superior hardness and resistance to abrasion, making them suitable for applications in automotive and industrial sectors. In contrast, polyether-based prepolymers are known for their excellent flexibility, low-temperature performance, and hydrolytic stability, ideal for flexible foams and coatings. The choice between these types depends on specific application requirements, including durability, flexibility, and environmental resistance, influencing their utilization in various industries.</p></p>
