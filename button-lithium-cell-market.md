<p><p>The Button Lithium Cell market comprises two main types: primary batteries and secondary batteries. Primary button cells are non-rechargeable, designed for single-use applications like watches, hearing aids, and small electronic devices. They offer long shelf life and stable voltage. On the other hand, secondary button cells are rechargeable, making them suitable for devices that require frequent use and longer operational life, such as certain medical instruments and high-drain electronics. Both types cater to specific market needs based on application and functionality.</p></p>
