<p><ul><li>Dispersion Method</li><li>Suspension Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PTFE grease market is primarily categorized into two types based on their manufacturing processes: dispersion method and suspension method. The dispersion method involves uniformly distributing PTFE particles in a thickening agent, resulting in a stable grease suitable for various applications. Conversely, the suspension method uses a liquid medium to suspend PTFE particles, allowing for enhanced lubrication properties. Both methods cater to diverse industrial needs, with variations in viscosity, temperature resistance, and performance, making them suitable for specific lubrication applications.</p></p>
