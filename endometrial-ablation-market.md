<p><p>The endometrial ablation market comprises various techniques used to treat abnormal uterine bleeding by removing or destroying the endometrial lining. Cryoablation utilizes extreme cold, while electrical ablation employs electrical currents. Hydrothermal ablation uses heated fluid, while hysteroscopy devices visualize the uterus during the procedure. Microwave endometrial ablation applies microwave energy, and radiofrequency ablation uses radio waves to achieve results. Thermal balloon ablation involves heating a balloon inside the uterus. Other methods may also be present, expanding treatment options.</p></p>
