<p><p>The medical atherectomy system market encompasses various types of atherectomy technologies designed to remove atherosclerotic plaques from blood vessels. Targeted atherectomy focuses on specific lesions using specialized catheters. Orbital atherectomy utilizes a rotating abrasive crown to effectively treat plaques in larger vessels. Photoablative (laser) atherectomy employs laser energy to vaporize the obstructive tissue, while rotational atherectomy involves a high-speed rotating burr to grind away plaque. Each type offers unique benefits and applications in vascular interventions.</p></p>
