<p><p>Wet rotor circulator pumps are categorized into vertical and horizontal designs. Horizontal wet rotor circulator pumps are installed in a horizontal position, making them ideal for larger systems requiring stable flow and pressure. Both types utilize a wet rotor design, where the motor is cooled by the pumped fluid, enhancing energy efficiency and reducing noise levels.</p></p>
