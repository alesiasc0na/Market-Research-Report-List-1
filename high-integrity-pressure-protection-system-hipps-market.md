<p><p>A High-Integrity Pressure Protection System (HIPPS) enhances safety by preventing overpressure scenarios in industrial applications. It comprises several components: Field Initiators detect pressure changes and initiate safety measures; Logic Solvers process input signals to determine actions; Valves control the flow of materials in the system based on commands from the logic solvers; and Actuators physically operate the valves in response to safety signals. This cohesive operation ensures reliable pressure management and protects equipment and personnel from potential hazards.</p></p>
