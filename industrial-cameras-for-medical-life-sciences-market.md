<p><strong>The Industrial Cameras for Medical & Life Sciences Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Area Scan Camera</li><li>Line Scan Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial cameras in the medical and life sciences market primarily include area scan and line scan cameras. Area scan cameras capture complete two-dimensional images at once, making them ideal for applications requiring detailed visualization of static samples, such as tissue analysis. In contrast, line scan cameras capture one-dimensional image lines sequentially, which is beneficial for inspecting moving samples, such as blood flow or curved surfaces. Both types are essential for enhancing imaging accuracy and efficiency in medical diagnostics and research.</p></p>
