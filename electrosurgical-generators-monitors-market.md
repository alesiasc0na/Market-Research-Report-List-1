<p><p>Electrosurgical generators and monitors are essential in surgical procedures, enabling tissue cutting and coagulation through high-frequency electrical currents. Monopole electrosurgical generators use a single active electrode to deliver energy, suitable for various soft tissue surgeries. Bipolar electrosurgical generators utilize two electrodes in close proximity, offering precise control and minimizing collateral damage, ideal for delicate procedures. Vessel sealing generators specifically target blood vessels, utilizing advanced technology to seal vessels effectively, reducing bleeding during surgical interventions. Together, they enhance surgical efficiency and safety.</p></p>
