<p><strong>The Radar Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pulsed Radar</li><li>Continuous Wave (CW) Radar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radar systems are primarily categorized into two market types: Pulsed Radar and Continuous Wave (CW) Radar. Pulsed Radar transmits short bursts of energy, allowing for precise range measurement and target detection, making it ideal for applications like air traffic control and weather monitoring. Continuous Wave Radar, on the other hand, emits a constant signal, enabling robust speed detection and tracking of moving objects, often used in traffic enforcement and automotive applications. Each type serves distinct operational needs across various industries.</p></p>
