<p><p>The COVID-19 test kit market comprises two primary types: Nucleic Acid Test Kits and Antibody Test Kits. Nucleic Acid Test Kits, commonly using PCR technology, detect the virus's genetic material, offering high accuracy for active infections. In contrast, Antibody Test Kits identify antibodies produced in response to the virus, indicating past infections. While Nucleic Acid Tests are crucial for diagnosing current cases, Antibody Tests help in understanding population exposure and potential immunity levels, both vital for managing the pandemic.</p></p>
